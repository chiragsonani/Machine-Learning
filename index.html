Linear Regression

Linear regression is a technique to fit a line through data points.

Mean Squared Error (MSE):

\[ \text{MSE} = \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^{N} (y_i - \hat{y}_i)^2 \]

Closed Form Solution:

Slope (\(m\)): \[ m = \frac{\sum \left( x - \bar{x} \right) \left( y - \bar{y} \right)}{\sum \left( x - \bar{x} \right)^2} \]

Intercept (\(b\)): \[ b = \bar{y} - m \bar{x} \]
